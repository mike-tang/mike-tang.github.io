JobQuest

Always be prepared for your dream job interview

What is JobQuest?

Whenever a friend complains to me about her/his career, I always respond with, "So what are you going to do about it?" There are two options: accept a state of complacency, or aspire to discover and pursue a true passion.

JobQuest provides a medium for people to act upon their will and discover a career worth pursuing. The product aims to provide job seekers with a simple way to gain insight on different career paths, cull wisdom from experienced professionals, and get the best preparation possible for their dream job interview.

My Role[s]

Sole designer and front end developer in a team of two. My scope covers all aspects of design: product, interface, visual, logo, web, and everything in between.

The Challenge

Design all aspects of the JobQuest user experience: the interface, user interactions, visual elements, and most importantly, a product that users could credit the beginnings of their dream career journeys to.
