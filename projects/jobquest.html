What is JobQuest?

Career decisions aren't easy. It's common to feel lost in the myriad of uncertainties that come with pursuing a new job or career path, especially when its consequences have a direct impact on quality of life.

JobQuest was a design experiment that a friend and I decided to take on as a side project. Our goal was to design a platform for job seekers to gain insight on different career paths and cull wisdom from experienced professionals so that they could make better career decisions for themselves.

My Role

Sole designer and front end developer in a team of two. My scope covered all aspects of design: product, interface, visual, logo, web, and everything in between.
