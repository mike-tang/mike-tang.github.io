Kuddly for iOS

Mobile marketplace for veterinarians and pet caretakers to connect and discuss everyday pet health questions

Overview

When pet caretakers encounter irregular behavior from their pets, they usually only have two options: wait to see if the problem goes away, or take them to the vet. Waiting to see if the problem eventually subsides is risky and worrisome. Alternatively, taking a pet to the vet can be expensive, inconvenient, and especially frustrating when a diagnosis reveals no problems at all.

Kuddly seeks to mitigate this problem for pet caretakers. The service allows them to connect directly with veterinarians through its mobile app and address everyday concerns regarding pet health and wellness.

My Role

Product designer. User experience and interface design lead. I provided design direction for the beta version of Kuddly's iOS app.

iOS App Design

It's important to keep a product's experience consistent across platforms (e.g. web, mobile, Android, iOS) while also adapting to the design conventions of each platform. With this principle in mind, I designed Kuddly's iOS app after completing mockups for the Android app and adapted as many features as possible to iOS 7's design guidelines.
