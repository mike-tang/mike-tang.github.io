Kuddly Logo

Mobile marketplace for veterinarians and pet caretakers to connect and discuss everyday pet health questions

Overview

When pet caretakers encounter irregular behavior from their pets, they usually only have two options: wait to see if the problem goes away, or take them to the vet. Waiting to see if the problem eventually subsides is risky and worrisome, and taking a pet to the vet can be expensive and inconvenient.

Kuddly seeks to mitigate this problem for pet caretakers. The service allows them to connect directly with veterinarians through its mobile app and address everyday questions about pet health and wellness.
