The Energy Coalition

Non-profit sustainability consultancy for local municipalities and communities

Overview

The Energy Coalition is a non-profit sustainability consultancy that helps local governments engage their communities with programs that promote accountable energy practices. I was asked to come up with a design concept for their new website and develop a prototype that would deliver content more efficiently to potential clients and rebrand the company with a fresh look.

My Role

User experience design, interaction design, visual design, responsive design, Wordpress template development

Information Architecture

One of the first necessary steps was to analyze the current site's navigation and content structure. I created a site map to help the team understand the fragmentation of their current site. In the streamlined version of the information architecture, I proposed reducing the 55 current pages down to five.
