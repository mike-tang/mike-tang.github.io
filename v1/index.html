Los Angeles County

Interactive Map Design

THE PROBLEM

LA County needed a way to visualize the impact and distribution of their energy efficiency projects. Project data needed to be consolidated into one central source and accessible across multiple consultant teams.

THE SOLUTION

I conceptualized and created a real-time, interactive map that sourced its data from Google Docs spreadsheets (collaboration ftw!). I set up a spreadsheet for each map layer, let team members input their data, and then watched all of it auto-populate into the map (hosted in Geocommons).
